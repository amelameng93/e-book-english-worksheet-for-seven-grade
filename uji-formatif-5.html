📋 Uji Formatif Bab 5

Pronoun and Noun

📋 Bagian A: Pilihan Ganda (10 soal, skor 10/soal) | ✍️ Bagian B: Essay (5 soal, skor 20/soal)

Materi: Jenis-jenis noun (common, proper, concrete, abstract, countable, uncountable, collective), jenis-jenis pronoun (personal, demonstrative, reflexive, interrogative, indefinite), serta penggunaan subject dan object pronoun.

Waktu: Tidak terbatas | Total Skor: 200 poin

📋 Data Siswa

📋 Bagian A – Pilihan Ganda

⚠️ Lengkapi semua 10 soal pilihan ganda! Setiap soal bernilai 10 poin.

Soal 1: Which of the following is a proper noun?

Soal 2: "Happiness" is an example of a/an ___ noun.

Soal 3: Which sentence uses the correct subject pronoun?

Soal 4: "A flock of birds" — the word "flock" is a ___ noun.

Soal 5: Which of the following is an uncountable noun?

Soal 6: "I made the cake ___." The correct reflexive pronoun is...

Soal 7: "___ is your name?" The correct interrogative pronoun is...

Soal 8: "This is my book. That is ___ book." (referring to your book)

Soal 9: "Everyone ___ happy today." The correct verb is...

Soal 10: Which sentence is grammatically correct?

✍️ Bagian B – Essay

⚠️ Isi Nama Lengkap dan NIS terlebih dahulu! Setiap soal essay bernilai 20 poin.

Soal Essay 1: Jelaskan perbedaan antara common noun dan proper noun, serta berikan masing-masing tiga contoh!

Soal Essay 2: Sebutkan dan jelaskan lima jenis pronoun yang Anda pelajari, serta berikan masing-masing satu contoh dalam kalimat!

Soal Essay 3: Lengkapi tabel berikut dengan pronoun yang tepat!
a) Subject pronoun untuk perempuan = ...
b) Object pronoun untuk "I" = ...
c) Possessive adjective untuk "they" = ...
d) Reflexive pronoun untuk "he" = ...
e) Possessive pronoun untuk "we" = ...

Soal Essay 4: Identifikasi semua noun dalam kalimat berikut dan tentukan jenisnya (common/proper, concrete/abstract, countable/uncountable):
"The students at MTsN 6 Agam showed great enthusiasm in learning English."

Soal Essay 5: Perbaiki kesalahan grammar dalam kalimat-kalimat berikut dan jelaskan perbaikannya!
a) "Me and my friend goes to the library."
b) "Her is a smart student."
c) "This is mine book."
d) "Everyone are happy today."
e) "The cat hurt it self."
